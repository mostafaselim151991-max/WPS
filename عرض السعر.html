Scope of Work
The scope of work generally includes of the following:
1. Collecting information
2. Conducting visits to the site to collect information about present land use, surface topography, geological features and surface drainage.
3. Drilling of the required boreholes by using rotary drilling rig.
4. Conducting Boring hole & SPT according ASTM specifications.
5. Description of subsurface formation
6. Carrying out the necessary field and laboratory tests according ASTM.
7. Collecting groundwater sample by Grab method.
8. Give design parameters for field and laboratory findings.
9. Excavation and dewatering primary recommendation
10. Propose recommendations for the foundation type and bearing capacity.
